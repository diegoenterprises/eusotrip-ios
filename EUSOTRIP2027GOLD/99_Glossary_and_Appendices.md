## A. Glossary of terms and acronyms

### Regulatory and compliance

- **AAR** — Association of American Railroads. Publishes Bill of Lading Format 5A.
- **ACE** — Automated Commercial Environment. US CBP's single window for imports and exports. e-Manifest 1 hour before arrival.
- **ACI** — Advanced Commercial Information. CBSA (Canada) equivalent of ACE. Mandatory 1 hour before arrival.
- **AIS** — Automatic Identification System. Maritime transponder tracking.
- **APHIS** — Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (USDA). Livestock inspections.
- **BASIC** — Behavior Analysis Safety Improvement Categories. Seven FMCSA scoring buckets.
- **BOC-3** — Designation of process agents for carrier authority per state. FMCSA requirement.
- **BOL** — Bill of Lading. Legal shipping document.
- **BOL-5A** — AAR uniform straight bill of lading for rail.
- **Carta Porte** — Mexican SAT CFDI 4.0 complement. Digital bill of lading for domestic MX transport.
- **CBP** — US Customs and Border Protection.
- **CBSA** — Canada Border Services Agency.
- **CDL** — Commercial Driver's License.
- **CDL-A / CDL-B** — Class A and Class B commercial licenses, distinguishing by GVWR and combination.
- **CFDI** — Comprobante Fiscal Digital por Internet. Mexican tax invoice standard.
- **CFIA** — Canadian Food Inspection Agency.
- **CFR** — Code of Federal Regulations (US).
- **CSA** — Compliance, Safety, Accountability. FMCSA's safety rating program.
- **CTPAT** — Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism. Trusted-trader program.
- **CVI** — Certificate of Veterinary Inspection (livestock).
- **CVSA** — Commercial Vehicle Safety Alliance. Level 1–8 inspection standards.
- **DG / DGD** — Dangerous Goods / Dangerous Goods Declaration.
- **DOT** — Department of Transportation (US).
- **DVIR** — Driver Vehicle Inspection Report. FMCSA-mandated pre-trip and post-trip inspection.
- **ELD** — Electronic Logging Device. Required since Dec 2017 for US commercial drivers.
- **ERG** — Emergency Response Guidebook. US DOT emergency response reference for hazmat.
- **FAST** — Free and Secure Trade. Expedited commercial border lane (US/CA, US/MX).
- **FDA** — Food and Drug Administration.
- **FMCSA** — Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration.
- **FRA** — Federal Railroad Administration.
- **FSMA** — Food Safety Modernization Act. Sanitary Transportation Rule at 21 CFR Part 1 Subpart O.
- **GVWR** — Gross Vehicle Weight Rating.
- **HazMat** — Hazardous Materials.
- **HM-232** — DOT security plan regulation for hazmat carriers + offerors.
- **HME** — Hazardous Materials Endorsement on CDL.
- **HMSP** — Hazmat Safety Permit (FMCSA).
- **HOS** — Hours of Service. 49 CFR Part 395.
- **IFTA** — International Fuel Tax Agreement. Quarterly per-jurisdiction fuel tax filing.
- **IMDG** — International Maritime Dangerous Goods Code. IMO hazmat regulations for ocean.
- **IMMEX** — Mexican export-processing maquila program.
- **IRP** — International Registration Plan. Apportioned plates for interstate commercial.
- **ISF 10+2** — Importer Security Filing. 10 importer + 2 carrier data elements filed 24h before ocean lading.
- **ISPS** — International Ship and Port Facility Security Code.
- **LCL** — Less than Container Load (ocean).
- **LTL** — Less Than Truckload.
- **MBL** — Master Bill of Lading (ocean, carrier to NVOCC).
- **MCN / MC** — Motor Carrier Number. FMCSA operating authority.
- **MVR** — Motor Vehicle Record. Driver history report.
- **NHS** — National Highway System. Hazmat-designated routes.
- **NMFC** — National Motor Freight Classification. LTL commodity class codes 50–500.
- **NMFTA** — National Motor Freight Traffic Association.
- **NOM** — Normas Oficiales Mexicanas. Mexican national standards.
- **NRC** — Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
- **NSC** — National Safety Code (Canada).
- **NVOCC** — Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier.
- **OOS** — Out of Service (truck, trailer, driver).
- **OTIF** — On Time In Full.
- **PARS** — Pre-Arrival Review System (CBSA). Northbound truck customs barcode.
- **Pedimento** — Mexican customs entry/exit declaration.
- **PHMSA** — Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (US).
- **PHMSA HMR** — Hazardous Materials Regulations at 49 CFR 171–180.
- **PIP** — Partners in Protection. Canadian trusted-trader program (equivalent to C-TPAT).
- **POD** — Proof of Delivery.
- **PSM / PHA** — Process Safety Management / Process Hazard Analysis.
- **RODS** — Record of Duty Status. HOS log.
- **RQ** — Reportable Quantity (hazmat).
- **SAFER** — FMCSA's public carrier safety snapshot.
- **SAGARPA / SENASICA** — Mexican agricultural + food safety agencies.
- **SARS** — Simplified Automated Reporting System (CBSA express/courier).
- **SAT** — Servicio de Administración Tributaria. Mexican tax authority.
- **SCAC** — Standard Carrier Alpha Code.
- **SCT / SICT** — Secretaría de Comunicaciones y Transportes (now SICT). Mexican federal transportation agency.
- **SMS** — Safety Measurement System (FMCSA percentile-based scoring).
- **SOLAS** — Safety of Life at Sea. IMO regulations, including VGM amendment.
- **STCC** — Standard Transportation Commodity Code. 7-digit rail commodity identifier.
- **TDG** — Transportation of Dangerous Goods (Canada).
- **TWIC** — Transportation Worker Identification Credential. TSA port-access card.
- **UCR** — Unified Carrier Registration. Annual fee by fleet size.
- **UIIC** — Uniform Intermodal Interchange and Facilities Access Agreement.
- **UN number** — United Nations identification number for dangerous goods.
- **USDOT** — US Department of Transportation number. Carrier identifier.
- **USMCA / T-MEC** — United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement. Replaced NAFTA July 2020.
- **VGM** — Verified Gross Mass. SOLAS Chapter VI Regulation 2 requirement for ocean export containers.
- **VUCEM** — Ventanilla Única de Comercio Exterior Mexicano. Mexican single-window customs portal.

### Business and operations

- **AP / AR** — Accounts Payable / Accounts Receivable.
- **BCO** — Beneficial Cargo Owner (ocean).
- **DSO** — Days Sales Outstanding (broker receivables).
- **ETA** — Estimated Time of Arrival.
- **FCL** — Full Container Load (ocean).
- **FTL** — Full Truckload.
- **GAAP** — Generally Accepted Accounting Principles.
- **KYB / KYC** — Know Your Business / Know Your Customer.
- **MABD** — Must Arrive By Date (retailer compliance).
- **MCI** — Minimum Commitment Incentive.
- **MQC** — Minimum Quantity Commitment (ocean).
- **MSA** — Master Services Agreement.
- **OO / O/O** — Owner-Operator.
- **OEM** — Original Equipment Manufacturer.
- **P&D** — Pickup and Delivery.
- **RFP / RFQ** — Request For Proposal / Quote.
- **SOC 2** — Service Organization Controls 2 (audit standard).
- **TEU** — Twenty-foot Equivalent Unit (container).
- **WMS** — Warehouse Management System.

### Technical

- **AES** — Automated Export System (CBP).
- **APNs** — Apple Push Notification service.
- **AIS** — Automatic Identification System (vessel tracking).
- **DTE** — Device-to-Entity (watchOS).
- **EDI** — Electronic Data Interchange. Common transaction sets: 204 (tender), 214 (in-transit), 315 (ocean status), 322 (rail consist), 404 (rail tender), 856 (ASN), 990 (tender response).
- **FCM** — Firebase Cloud Messaging.
- **GTIN / SSCC** — Global Trade Item Number / Serial Shipping Container Code (GS1).
- **IdP** — Identity Provider.
- **JWT** — JSON Web Token.
- **LWW** — Last-Writer-Wins (conflict resolution).
- **MCP** — Model Context Protocol (Anthropic).
- **PAC** — Proveedor Autorizado de Certificación (Mexican SAT-authorized digital signature provider).
- **QCS** — Quay Crane Scheduling (terminal).
- **RBAC** — Role-Based Access Control.
- **RLM** — Recursive Language Model (this doctrine's self-improvement pattern). See [98_Recursive_Language_Model_Integration.md](./98_Recursive_Language_Model_Integration.md).
- **RLS** — Row-Level Security.
- **TOS** — Terminal Operating System.
- **tRPC** — Type-safe RPC framework. EusoTrip's primary API layer.
- **ULID** — Universally Unique Lexicographically Sortable Identifier.
